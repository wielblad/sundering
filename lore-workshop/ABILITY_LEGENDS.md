## IRONCLAD - The Unyielding Shield

### Q - SHIELD STRIKE
**Legendary Name:** *"The Defender's Answer"*

**Origin Tale:**
During the third night of defending the Northern Gate, Torvin grasped something fundamental. A demon struck his shield with the force of an avalanche, and Torvin... did not yield. Instead, he felt all the energy of the blow flow through the metal, through his body, and return. The demon was hurled back ten paces.

*"A shield is not a wall"* — he wrote later in his journal. *"A wall blocks. A shield answers."*

**Visual Effect:**
Ironclad's shield blazes with amber light, and at the moment of impact, a wave of energy erupts forth, stunning the foe. For a fleeting instant, the outlines of ancient protective runes shimmer in the air.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"You stand before me? Here is my answer!"*

---

### W - IRON FORTRESS
**Legendary Name:** *"The Covenant with Steel"*

**Origin Tale:**
Torvin's armor is no ordinary metal. During the Battle of the Northern Gate, when demonic energy sought to consume him, the armor... reacted. It absorbed the energy. Fused with his flesh. And learned to defend its bearer.

Now, when Torvin summons the Iron Fortress, the armor literally thickens. Metal flows from all across his body to wherever protection is needed most.

**Visual Effect:**
The armor gleams with metallic radiance, as additional layers of steel manifest upon its surface. Small metal fragments hover around Ironclad, as though the very metal itself comes to his aid.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"Steel shall not fail me. Steel never betrays."*

---

### E - CHARGE
**Legendary Name:** *"The Run of Last Hope"*

**Origin Tale:**
Everyone in the Northern Citadel knows the legend of Torvin's Run. When the gate fell, the only hope was to reach the Stormbringer Hammer — an artifact capable of repelling demons. Five hundred paces separated him from it, and a thousand enemies.

Torvin ran. He did not stop. He did not fight. He simply ran through the wall of demons, felling all who stood in his path.

They say that to this day, the Citadel walls bear the mark — a groove carved by his shield into the stone.

**Visual Effect:**
Ironclad lowers his stance, shield thrust forward, and behind him trails a streak of sparks and smoke. Enemies in his path are scattered aside as if struck by a battering ram.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"Out of my way. Or through you."*

---

### R - UNYIELDING WILL
**Legendary Name:** *"The Defender's Oath"*

**Origin Tale:**
On the final day of the Northern Gate's defense, Torvin stood alone. All other defenders had fallen. He stood in the gateway, surrounded by a mountain of corpses, as yet another wave marched toward him.

He fell to his knees. His ribs were shattered, his face slashed open, his shoulders wrenched. But then... he heard weeping behind him. Children hidden in the Citadel's cellars.

*"No"* — he said. And he rose.

For the next three hours, nothing could bring him down. No blow, no curse, no demon. His body refused to fall until the last child was evacuated.

**Visual Effect:**
Ironclad's armor erupts with golden light. His eyes blaze with white fire. For several seconds he seems larger, more powerful — as though the spirits of all defenders have entered his body.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"You may slay me. But I shall not move from this place!"*

## BLADEWARDEN - Storm of Steel

### Q - LIGHTNING CUT
**Legendary Name:** *"The First Dance"*

**Origin Tale:**
In the gladiator arena, Kael learned that most fights are decided in the first second. He who strikes first, wins. He who hesitates, dies.

"The First Dance" is a cut so swift that the eye barely registers it. Kael devised it during combat with a sword master who was twice his better. Kael could not defeat him in a fair fight. So he ensured the fight was not fair.

**Visual Effect:**
A blur of motion, then a flash of both blades. The wound appears on the opponent before the viewer comprehends what has occurred. Behind Kael lingers a spectral trace of his former position.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"Grace or Vengeance? It matters not — both are swifter than you."*

---

### W - BLADE DANCE
**Legendary Name:** *"The Song of Blood"*

**Origin Tale:**
There exists a forbidden technique of swordcraft called "The Song of Blood." Forbidden, because its execution demands complete surrender to combat — without fear, without thought, without mercy.

Kael learned it from a condemned man awaiting execution. The old master had one day left to live and chose to pass on his knowledge.

*"When the blood sings in your veins"* — he said — *"let it dance through the blades."*

**Visual Effect:**
Kael whirls in place, his blades forming a lethal circle of light. Anyone who enters this circle receives dozens of wounds. Around him hover droplets of blood — his own and his enemies'.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"Do you hear it? This is the song you have awaited all your life."*

---

### E - PARRY
**Legendary Name:** *"Shadow of Response"*

**Origin Tale:**
Every gladiator knows desperation. That moment when you know the blow is coming, and you cannot evade it. Most die in that moment.

Kael learned to transform desperation into opportunity. "Shadow of Response" is not a parry — it is a redirection. You receive the energy of the strike and return it with doubled force.

This demands perfect timing. A millisecond too early — the blow passes through your guard. A millisecond too late — the strike lands before the parry.

**Visual Effect:**
Kael's blades cross at the moment of the enemy's strike, forming an X of light. The opponent is thrown back, stunned by their own force turned against them.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"Your strength? Now mine."*

---

### R - STORM OF STEEL
**Legendary Name:** *"The Final Dance of Two Hundred"*

**Origin Tale:**
Kael never speaks of his last day in the arena. But the witnesses remember.

The arena's owner — his father — decided to "honor" his two hundredth victory. He released twenty gladiators at once. None were meant to leave alive.

What Kael did over the next three minutes passed into legend. Twenty bodies. Forty cuts. Not a single wound upon his flesh.

When the last opponent fell, Kael looked up at the stands where his father sat. He raised Grace.

And walked out through the main gate. No one tried to stop him.

**Visual Effect:**
Kael vanishes into a vortex of steel. For several seconds, only the flash of blades and fountains of blood are visible. When the storm subsides, enemies lie around him in a perfect circle.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"For all who fell for your amusement... HERE IS YOUR REWARD!"*

## SYLARA - The Life Giver

### Q - HEALING LIGHT
**Legendary Name:** *"Kiss of Dawn"*

**Origin Tale:**
When Sylara first saw a dying man, she did not understand what was happening. Why was his light fading? Why was the warmth fleeing?

Instinctively, she reached out her hand and... reminded him how to shine. It was not healing — it was remembering. Every living creature remembers how to be whole. Sylara simply awakens that memory.

**Visual Effect:**
From Sylara's palm flows gentle golden-green light. It touches the wounded like a sunbeam through leaves, and the body begins to glow from within, wounds closing like buds blooming in reversed time.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"Remember... who you were, before the world wounded you."*

---

### W - PURIFICATION AURA
**Legendary Name:** *"Breath of the Eternal Grove"*

**Origin Tale:**
In the heart of the Eternal Grove grows the Greatmother — a tree older than memory. Her breath purifies air, water, souls.

Sylara learned to echo that breath. When she calls upon it, for a moment she becomes an extension of the Greatmother — her leaves, her roots, her breath.

**Visual Effect:**
Around Sylara spreads a wave of golden-green light. Wherever it touches, poisons are neutralized, curses shatter like old cobwebs, and allies sense the fragrance of a spring forest.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"I breathe for you. I breathe with you. I breathe through you."*

---

### E - NATURE'S EMBRACE
**Legendary Name:** *"The Clasp of Roots"*

**Origin Tale:**
Even beings of light must defend themselves. When Sylara was first attacked, the shrub beside her came alive. It wrapped itself around the assailant and held him until the danger passed.

Since then, Sylara learned to ask plants for aid. They always answer. The forest remembers those who love it.

**Visual Effect:**
Luminous roots rise from the earth, coiling around the enemy's legs. The plants seem alive — pulsing with green energy and whispering in an unknown tongue.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"The forest remembers. The forest protects. The forest... does not release."*

---

### R - TREE OF LIFE
**Legendary Name:** *"Miracle of the Greatmother"*

**Origin Tale:**
Once every thousand years, the Greatmother bears fruit. One single fruit. Whoever consumes it cannot die for one day and one night.

Sylara does not know the Greatmother's secret. But she learned something similar — for a few moments, she can create an echo of the Greatmother, a fragment of her power.

It drains her almost entirely. But when she sees allies rise from wounds that should have slain them... she knows it is worth the cost.

**Visual Effect:**
The earth cracks and an immense, luminous tree rises. Its crown shines like the sun, and its branches reach toward the wounded. Golden pollen drifts from its leaves, healing every wound it touches.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"Greatmother, lend me your light. Let those who fight for life know not death this day!"*

## VEX - The Venomous Huntress

### Q - POISONED DAGGER
**Legendary Name:** *"Mother's Kiss"*

**Origin Tale:**
Vex's mother healed with poison. It sounds paradoxical, but in the slums, anything can be medicine — given the proper dose.

*"Poison and remedy are the same substance"* — she would say. *"The difference is intent."*

Vex took this lesson to heart. Her poisons kill swiftly and painlessly — because her mother taught her that suffering is unnecessary.

**Visual Effect:**
Vex's dagger gleams with a greenish glow. When it strikes true, dark veins of poison spread from the wound, pulsing in rhythm with the victim's heart.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"From my mother I learned to heal. From the world — to kill."*

---

### W - ACROBATIC DODGE
**Legendary Name:** *"Dance of Slum Shadows"*

**Origin Tale:**
In the slums beneath the arena, space is more precious than gold. Corridors are narrow, ceilings low, and pursuit can come from any direction.

Vex learned to move through this labyrinth like a fish through water. Leaping across walls, slipping through gaps, vanishing into shadows others did not know existed.

**Visual Effect:**
Vex dissolves into the darkness, her silhouette blurring like smoke. She appears elsewhere — behind backs, overhead, wherever no one thinks to look.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"You cannot see me. No one ever has."*

---

### E - PARALYZING STRIKE
**Legendary Name:** *"The Final Breath"*

**Origin Tale:**
There are places on the body where even a small dose of poison causes complete paralysis. Vex knows every one of them.

She learned anatomy from the only medic in the slums — a drunkard who had once been a palace surgeon. For a bottle of gin, he taught her where the nerves lie, where the arteries run, where life hangs by a thread.

**Visual Effect:**
Vex strikes with surgical precision. From the point of impact spreads a wave of pale green light, and the victim freezes in place, unable to move.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"Feel that tingling? That is the last thing you will ever feel."*

---

### R - DEATH PURSUIT
**Legendary Name:** *"The Oath of the Slums"*

**Origin Tale:**
When Vex's mother lay dying, Vex swore that none responsible would escape. She hunted everyone who contributed to the Plague's outbreak in the slums. Arms dealers. Guards who took bribes. Aristocrats who blocked aid.

Thirteen names. Thirteen deaths.

Since then, when Vex marks a target, nothing can stop her. They may hide. They may flee. But she will find them regardless.

**Visual Effect:**
Vex's eyes burn with a green glow. Upon her target appears a mark invisible to others — and from that moment, every step she takes brings her closer to the quarry. Shadows seem to point her way.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"I chased you through hell itself. Do you think I will stop here?"*

## MAGNUS - Master of Gravity

### Q - GRAVITY SPHERE
**Legendary Name:** *"Memory of the Mistake"*

**Origin Tale:**
Each time Magnus creates a gravity sphere, for a fraction of a second he sees their faces. Forty magisters. His mentor. His friend.

The micro-black hole he accidentally created looked exactly the same. Only... it did not vanish.

Now he controls this power. But the memory never fades.

**Visual Effect:**
A black sphere forms in the air, encircled by a swirling ring of light. Everything around it — dust, light, sound — bends and falls into its center.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"I see you. I always see you. And I shall never cease my apology."*

---

### W - GRAVITY FIELD
**Legendary Name:** *"The Law of Falling"*

**Origin Tale:**
Gravity has its laws. Magnus rewrote them.

Under normal conditions, everything falls downward. But "down" is merely a direction — a direction Magnus can alter.

*"Imagine"* — he once explained to a student — *"that the ground is suddenly there."* He pointed at the wall. The student fell against the wall and fainted from shock.

**Visual Effect:**
The area around Magnus suddenly becomes "heavy." The air thickens, light bends, and enemies feel as though the entire world presses upon their shoulders.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"The law decrees: all things fall. The law does not specify: where."*

---

### E - GRAVITY LEAP
**Legendary Name:** *"Flight of the Fallen"*

**Origin Tale:**
Magnus does not fly. Magnus... redefines falling.

When "up" becomes "wherever I wish to be," reaching it is merely a matter of letting go. The body flies toward the new "ground" on its own.

Side effect: after each such "leap," Magnus momentarily loses track of which way is up. It is disorienting.

**Visual Effect:**
Magnus rises into the air, surrounded by swirling equations and symbols. Then he launches in his chosen direction, leaving behind a trail of refracted light.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"Gravity is a suggestion. And I am very persuasive."*

---

### R - BLACK HOLE
**Legendary Name:** *"Echo of Catastrophe"*

**Origin Tale:**
It is the same power. Exactly the same one he accidentally unleashed forty years ago.

The difference? Now Magnus controls it. Now he knows how to create it, how to sustain it, how to close it.

But every time he summons it, part of him fears that this time he will fail to contain it. That history will repeat itself.

**Visual Effect:**
In the center of the battlefield, a miniature black hole opens. Light bends, space warps, and everything nearby begins to slide inexorably toward the center. Enemies are drawn in and crushed by an unstoppable force.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"I have seen the end of the world. It was black. And silent. And very, very close."*

## GRIMJAW - The Berserker

### Q - BLOODSTRIKE
**Legendary Name:** *"Gift of the Plague"*

**Origin Tale:**
Grok's blood is poison. Literally. The Shadow Plague mutated his body, transforming him into a walking reservoir of toxins.

When he bleeds, the poison is released. And instead of weakening him... it strengthens him. His muscles swell, his reflexes sharpen, pain vanishes.

This is not magic. This is adaptation. Grok learned to become the poison that lives.

**Visual Effect:**
Grok strikes with an axe that gleams with a dark crimson glow. Upon impact, blood — his and his victim's — swirls through the air like a living creature.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"BLOOD! MORE BLOOD!"*

---

### W - WARRIOR'S FRENZY
**Legendary Name:** *"Song of the North"*

**Origin Tale:**
The Blood Snow tribe knows no fear. This is not a boast — it is biological fact. Ages of life on the frozen wastelands removed fear from their very essence.

When Grok enters the Frenzy, he becomes what his ancestors always were: pure, unstoppable destruction.

**Visual Effect:**
Grok's eyes glow blood-red. His muscles bulge, veins pulse with dark blood, and from his throat erupts a wolf's howl.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"GROK KNOWS NO FEAR! GROK IS FEAR!"*

---

### E - DEMON JAW
**Legendary Name:** *"Trophy of the First Kill"*

**Origin Tale:**
Grok tore this jaw from a demon's maw with his bare hands. He was sixteen years old.

The demon was twice his size. Stronger. Faster. But it made a mistake — it attacked Grok's tribe.

For three days, Grok pursued the beast through the snowdrifts. Without weapons. Without sleep. Without food. And then, when the demon finally stopped...

The jaw still hangs around Grok's neck to this day. And it still holds power.

**Visual Effect:**
Grok summons the demon's spirit. A spectral, blazing jaw appears in the air and devours everything in its path.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"DEMON TRIED TO EAT GROK. GROK ATE DEMON!"*

---

### R - IMMORTAL FURY
**Legendary Name:** *"The Berserker's Final Dance"*

**Origin Tale:**
The Legend of the North speaks of the Final Dance — a state in which a berserker becomes so enraged that death itself fears to claim him.

Grok achieved this state once. During a siege of his settlement, when all seemed lost. For two hours he fought alone against a hundred. And when the last enemy fell, Grok had seventeen arrows in his body, three swords, and a hatchet.

He lived. Barely. But he lived.

**Visual Effect:**
Grok's body blazes with a dark crimson aura. Wounds do not bleed — the blood instantly returns to his body. Every strike against him makes him stronger, faster, more invincible.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"GROK DOES NOT DIE! GROK KILLS! AND KILLS! AND KILLS UNTIL NOTHING STANDS!"*

## VALERIA - The Radiant Shield

### Q - HOLY STRIKE
**Legendary Name:** *"Fist of Dawn"*

**Origin Tale:**
The Order of Dawn believes that light is the purest weapon. It does not kill — it purifies. It does not wound — it removes darkness.

Valeria learned to condense the light of morning into her blade. When she strikes, it is not metal that cuts flesh — it is light that burns away darkness.

**Visual Effect:**
Valeria's sword flashes with blinding radiance. The blow leaves a trail of light in the air, and where it lands, the enemy's skin glows briefly from within.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"Through light. For light. In light."*

---

### W - SHIELD OF LIGHT
**Legendary Name:** *"Blessing of the Order"*

**Origin Tale:**
During the Rite of Purification, every knight of the Order receives a blessing. A ray of light touches their brow, and from that moment they may summon the Shield — a barrier of pure faith.

Valeria remembers her Rite. She remembers how the light entered her and... remained. Since then, she has been part of something greater.

**Visual Effect:**
Before Valeria materializes a translucent shield of concentrated light. Attacks deflect from it with the sound of a cathedral bell.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"Faith is my shield. Faith shall not fail me."*

---

### E - JUDGMENT OF LIGHT
**Legendary Name:** *"Gaze of Truth"*

**Origin Tale:**
Valeria's eyes see more than they should. During the vision at her Rite... something changed. Now she can see the darkness in hearts — literally.

When she focuses on an enemy, the light within burns brighter. And darkness... darkness cannot abide the light.

**Visual Effect:**
Valeria's eyes gleam gold. A beam of light connects her to the target, and around the enemy appears a halo — the darker the soul, the brighter it burns.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"I see what you are. And the light shall judge you."*

---

### R - PURIFYING BEAM
**Legendary Name:** *"Wrath of the Heavens"*

**Origin Tale:**
Legend tells that the first Grand Master of the Order called upon the heavens for aid during the Battle of a Thousand Demons. The sun answered — and half the enemy army ceased to exist.

Valeria does not know if this is true. But she knows that when she truly needs it, when faith is absolute, when there is not a shadow of doubt... the sun listens.

**Visual Effect:**
The sky parts. A column of pure, white light descends from above like the hammer of the gods. Everything within its reach is flooded with purifying radiance — enemies burn, allies are healed.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"HEAVENS, HEAR MY PRAYER! LET LIGHT PURIFY THIS WORLD!"*

## HEX - The Curse Weaver

### Q - CURSE OF AGONY
**Legendary Name:** *"Memory of Fire"*

**Origin Tale:**
Hex remembers how she burned. Every detail. The smell of her own skin. The sound of her own scream. What it was like when the fire entered her.

Now she shares this memory. Everyone she curses feels the same — without wounds, without fire, but with the full measure of pain.

*"I want you to understand"* — she sometimes says. *"Just for a moment. So you know what you did to me."*

**Visual Effect:**
Black flames coil around the target. The victim screams, though no injuries appear on their body — the pain is purely mental, yet equally real.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"I burned. Now it is your turn."*

---

### W - CURSE OF WEAKNESS
**Legendary Name:** *"Echo of Betrayal"*

**Origin Tale:**
When Hex was burned the second time, her own neighbors brought the wood. People she had helped. People she had healed. People whose cats she had fed.

Betrayal weakens. Literally. Since then, Hex learned to curse trust — to make the body refuse obedience, as though it betrayed itself.

**Visual Effect:**
Around the target appear spectral chains — the hands of those who loved them, pulling them down. The victim weakens, as if their own body has turned against them.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"Your loved ones. Your friends. Where are they now? Nowhere. Like mine."*

---

### E - CURSED GROUND
**Legendary Name:** *"The Burning Ground"*

**Origin Tale:**
The place where they burned Hex remains cursed to this day. Nothing grows there. Birds avoid it. People have nightmares.

Hex learned to carry this curse with her. Wherever she stands, she can momentarily transform the earth into an echo of that charred ground.

**Visual Effect:**
The earth blackens and cracks. From the fissures rises gray smoke and spectral flames. Everything living within this area weakens, as though the very earth drains life from it.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"This is where I died. Three times. Join me."*

---

### R - ULTIMATE CURSE
**Legendary Name:** *"The Third Return"*

**Origin Tale:**
Three times they burned her. Three times she rose. The third time... something entered her. Something that should not exist.

When Hex invokes the Ultimate Curse, she briefly allows that something to emerge. And that something... very much wishes to share its pain.

**Visual Effect:**
Hex rises into the air, her eyes turning black as night. Around her swirl spirits — not her spirits, but spirits of something older, hungrier. Everything around blackens, dies, rots.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"THREE TIMES YOU SLEW ME. AND THREE TIMES I RETURNED. NOW YOU SHALL SEE WHAT CAME BACK WITH ME."*

## PYRALIS - The Flame Incarnate

### Q - FIREBALL
**Legendary Name:** *"Kiss of the Sun"*

**Origin Tale:**
When Pyralis was a young flame — yes, flames have youth — he dreamed of the sun. That distant, immense light in the sky that was... like a father? Mother? Source?

The first fireball he created was an attempt to imitate the sun. A small, swirling fragment of a star. Imperfect. But beautiful.

**Visual Effect:**
A sphere of pure fire, spinning like a miniature sun. It leaves behind a trail of smoke and sparks. When it strikes, it explodes in a cascade of flames.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"The sun dreams of touching the world. I am its dream."*

---

### W - FIRE WAVE
**Legendary Name:** *"Breath of the Volcano"*

**Origin Tale:**
Pyralis was born in the heart of a volcano. His first breath was an eruption that killed thousands.

He did not know. He did not understand. Fire does not think of consequences — it simply burns.

Now, when he exhales fire, he tries to... control it. Direct it. But part of him is still that innocent volcano that did not know it was killing.

**Visual Effect:**
Pyralis opens what resembles a mouth and unleashes a cone of flames. The air ripples with heat, metal melts, and sand transforms to glass.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"I breathe. The world burns. I am sorry. And I am not sorry."*

---

### E - FLAME SPRINT
**Legendary Name:** *"Dance of Flame"*

**Origin Tale:**
Fire dances. It always dances. It does not stand still, does not pause — it vibrates, leaps, gambols.

Pyralis learned that movement is life. When he moves, he is nearly impossible to strike — for how does one strike a flame?

**Visual Effect:**
Pyralis dissolves into a shower of sparks and appears elsewhere. For an instant he is everywhere and nowhere — pure motion, pure fire.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"Catch me. Catch the wind. Catch a thought. Impossible? Yes."*

---

### R - INFERNO
**Legendary Name:** *"Heart of the Sun"*

**Origin Tale:**
Pyralis has never touched the sun. But he knows what it looks like within. He feels it. It is his family, his source, his destiny.

When he summons Inferno, for a moment he becomes the sun. Small, destructible, but... the sun.

It drains him. Nearly kills him. But for that one moment, he is what he has always wished to be.

**Visual Effect:**
Pyralis rises into the air, and around him forms a sphere of pure, white fire. The temperature climbs to impossible levels. Everything around burns, melts, vanishes.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"FOR ONE MOMENT... I AM THE SUN!"*

## NIGHTSHADE - The Shadow Reaper

### Q - SHADOW STRIKE
**Legendary Name:** *"Death Without Trace"*

**Origin Tale:**
In the Shadow Realm, she was taught that killing is an art. It is not about pain — it is about... perfection. A clean strike that ends life without needless suffering.

Nightshade takes pride that her victims rarely know they are dying. It is a gift. Mercy.

**Visual Effect:**
Nightshade dissolves into shadow and appears behind the target. One cut — so swift that the victim does not even feel pain. Only... the end.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"..."* (Nightshade does not speak during an attack. Words are noise.)

---

### W - CLOAK OF SHADOWS
**Legendary Name:** *"Embrace of Darkness"*

**Origin Tale:**
The shadows love her. Truly. It sounds strange, but for a being from the Shadow Realm, shadow is... home. Family. Protection.

When Nightshade summons the Cloak, shadows surround her like a loving mother embracing her child. No one sees her. No one knows. She is safe.

**Visual Effect:**
Shadows from all around draw toward Nightshade, wrapping her in a cloak of absolute darkness. She becomes invisible — even her breath vanishes.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"Home..."* (a barely audible whisper)

---

### E - FAN OF BLADES
**Legendary Name:** *"Rain of Sorrow"*

**Origin Tale:**
Sometimes... sometimes a clean kill is not possible. Sometimes there are too many. Sometimes chaos must be chosen.

Nightshade does not like this. Fan of Blades is a dirty technique — many blades, many wounds, no precision. But it is effective.

Every time she uses it, Nightshade thinks of that child who wept over her mother. And wonders how many such children she is creating.

**Visual Effect:**
Nightshade releases a fan of shadow blades — each appearing like solidified darkness. They glide through the air soundlessly, striking everything in their path.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"I do not want to. But I must."*

---

### R - MARK OF DEATH
**Legendary Name:** *"Promise of the End"*

**Origin Tale:**
In the Shadow Realm, there exists a ritual of marking. Once a target is marked, there is no escape. No shelter. Shadow will find them anywhere.

Nightshade uses this technique sparingly. For once she marks someone, she must kill them. It is not a choice — it is compulsion. The mark demands fulfillment.

**Visual Effect:**
Nightshade touches the target, leaving a mark invisible to others. From that moment, the target glows with dark radiance in her eyes. And no matter how far they flee — she sees them. Always.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"You have been chosen. I am sorry."*

## THORNWEAVER - The Living Fortress

### Q - THORN WHIP
**Legendary Name:** *"Wrath of the Grove"*

**Origin Tale:**
When Rowan became part of the Great Oak, he also inherited its wrath. Wrath that had built for centuries. Wrath against all who felled, burned, destroyed.

The Thorn Whip is the manifestation of that wrath — every thorn remembers a wound inflicted upon some tree. And every one wishes to repay the debt.

**Visual Effect:**
From Thornweaver's body grows a long, writhing tendril covered in sharp thorns. It lashes through the air like a whip, and every thorn leaves a deep, bleeding wound.

**Battle Incantation:**
*"Every thorn. Every wound. Remembered."*

---

### W - PROTECTIVE BARK
**Legendary Name:** *"A Thousand Rings"*

**Origin Tale:**
The Great Oak has a thousand rings — a thousand years of life inscribed in its body. Every ring is a year. Every year is a story.

Rowan learned to summon this history to the surface. His skin becomes bark, and the bark becomes a shield — unyielding, remembering, eternal.
