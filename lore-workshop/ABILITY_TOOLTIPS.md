# HUMAN ALLIANCE

---

## IRONCLAD - The Unbreakable Shield
*Tank*

### Q - SHIELD BASH
**Slams shield into target enemy, dealing physical damage and stunning them briefly.**
*"A shield is not a wall. A wall blocks. A shield answers."*

### W - IRON FORTRESS
**Raises shield defensively, blocking incoming damage and reducing all damage taken for a duration.**
*"Steel shall not fail me. Steel never betrays."*

### E - CHARGE
**Charges forward in a line, knocking aside enemies and stopping at the first champion hit, dealing physical damage.**
*"Out of my way. Or through you."*

### R - UNYIELDING WILL
**Becomes immune to all crowd control effects and gains massive armor for 5 seconds.**
*"You may slay me. But I shall not move from this place."*
